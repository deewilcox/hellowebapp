About The Workroom

Mission

The Workroom is a makerspace dedicated to nurturing creativity and curiosity in the kids and families of East Nashville.

Vision

Our vision is to offer a space to work, to tinker, to dream, and to learn. We will offer physical work space, as well as tools and instruction.

Community

We are proud to be a part of and to serve the East Nashville community.
